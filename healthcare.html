Patient Privacy and HIPAA Compliance

Transmitting medical info over the internet, (i.e., when a patient fills out health forms online before an appointment), saves time and reduces errors. Since the advent of HIPAA and ePHI regulations, employees must follow strict protocols when handling an individual’s health information online.

HIPAA compliance regarding secure transmission and storage of data may include the following factors:

  • Enterprise-level hosting including staging sites, secure data collection and storage, and HIPAA firewalls
  • Encryption, permissions and procedures for emails and handling data
  • Patient portals, opt-in forms, and medical forms
  • Data integration with healthcare CRMs, EMRs and patient registries
  • EPIC data imports
  • Private and secure development server for staging updates for review
  • One-click push to production from staging server, with automatic backups, via our proprietary SYNC Staging platform
  • Integration with third-party portals (human resources, investor relations, news feeds, and more)
