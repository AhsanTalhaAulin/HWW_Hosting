WordPress Website Development

Keeping pace with the changing world of how customers get information and make choices, business owners now understand the importance of having a professional website, and may even be looking to update an existing site make it easy for people to use it on their phones. Website styles and functionality have entered a new phase of expectations, and WordPress is a great tool for making it simple for customers to find a business online, learn about it, and take action. In addition, WordPress provides a robust and reliable content management system (CMS) to use for managing and changing the website as needed.

Harris Web Works can handle all aspects of WordPress website development and management:

  • Design, code and build the site
  • Populate the site with content provided by you or created with our copy-writing help
  • Administer the site – manage ongoing updates or hand over the reigns and train employees on best practices

Custom Design with Your Branding

Your website is your piece of property on the web. It’s important that it uniquely represents your company, strengthens your branding, and reflects your style. Starting with WordPress as the foundation and building it with a custom template means the site will have the look, feel and functionality you want, plus the ability to grow with your business. Design, branding and functionality won’t be restricted by the boundaries of a pre-set theme.

See Choosing a WordPress Theme for more info on custom themes.

Maximize Search Results

There are many factors that influence where a company lands in search results – when someone “googles” the company name or a topic related to it. One of the most important search engine optimization (SEO) strategies is to make sure the site has strong and relevant keywords, meta descriptions and long-tail URLs, along with targeted landing pages. We’ll add a search optimization plug-in (we like Yoast) to help administrators apply relevant keywords to attract people to the site.

Contact us today to discuss how we can help your business grow with a speedy, easy-to-update WordPress website, and related support services.
