Making a Complex eCommerce Store Successful

We apply all elements needed for an ecommerce website to succeed: a great shopping experience (UI/UX), a search friendly foundation, a well-branded design, and an efficient content management system (CMS). Further, we can integrate the Magento store with your NetSuite ERP system for a complete business solution (learn about SYNC ConnectionTM ). 

Our capabilities cover updating existing sites in Magento 1.x and building new sites in Magento 2.x, using Magento Commerce or Magento Open Source. Features may include:

  • Quick loading, easy to navigate and checkout
  • Scalability to easily add products and product lines
  • Integrated with NetSuite back-office ERP systems for accounting, supply, shipping, etc. (see SYNC Connection White Paper)
  • Efficient and secure transactions using credit cards or other payment methods such as PayPal
  • Drop-ship integration for selling products from other suppliers
  • Database integration to easily update data feeds and upload to shopping channels (Amazon, eBay, Bizrate, Google Shopping, Shopzilla, etc.)
  • Special custom features such as showing shoppers related products
