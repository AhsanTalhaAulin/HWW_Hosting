Website Administration

Keeping your website fresh and relevant is key to continually attracting new and repeat visitors. However, it can be a daunting chore and not within your core competencies. Our team of long-time administrators can help!

Acting as an extension of your company, our experienced, proficient website administrators will get to know your site and handle all content updates and technical needs.

We provide ongoing support for new and existing websites:

  • Magento ecommerce updates – our Magento-Certified team can make content updates, troubleshoot & fix issues that may arise, manage promotions, and implement site enhancements
  • WordPress, Joomla or Drupal corporate and service websites – our experienced administrators add new content, handle any technical issues that may arise, and work in tandem with you to continually improve the site

We’ll handle “behind-the-scenes” necessities such as security updates, software maintenance and version updates, SEO and more, to keep your site up and running as intended.
